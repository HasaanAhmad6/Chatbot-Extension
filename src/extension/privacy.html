Privacy Policy for RAG Site Explorer

Last updated: July 2026

RAG Site Explorer (the "Extension") is committed to protecting your privacy. This document outlines how the Extension handles information when you use it.

Key Takeaway: We do not run any backend servers. Your API keys, scraped website contents, and questions are stored locally on your device and sent directly to your configured AI provider. No data is sent to us or any third party under our control.

1. Information Gathered and How It Is Used

  • API Keys: The Extension requires you to provide your own API keys for AI models (such as Google Gemini, OpenAI, etc.). These keys are stored locally on your device using Chrome's secure storage API (`chrome.storage.local`). They are only used to make direct API requests from your browser to the respective AI provider.
  • Crawl Content: When you request the Extension to index a website, it fetches public pages from that website's domain, parses the HTML, and builds a search index. This index is stored entirely on your local device in `chrome.storage.local`.
  • Chat Questions and Context: When you ask a question, the Extension retrieves matching text chunks from your local storage and sends them along with your question directly to your configured AI provider to generate an answer.

2. Data Transmission

All network traffic is conducted directly between the Extension and the official APIs of the AI providers you configure (e.g. Google Generative Language API, OpenAI API). No database or server intercepts these requests.

3. Data Retention and Deletion

All stored data (keys, cached indexes) remains on your local browser. You can clear all cached data at any time by uninstalling the Extension or clicking "Clear Storage" in Chrome settings.

4. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this policy from time to time. Any changes will be reflected by the "Last updated" date at the top of this page.

5. Contact

If you have any questions about this privacy policy, please open an issue on our GitHub repository.
